Grade 7 Math Curriculum

Our Grade 7 Mathematics program is designed to build a strong foundation in key mathematical concepts while developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

The curriculum is aligned with national standards and covers five major domains:

  • Ratios and Proportional Relationships
  • The Number System
  • Expressions and Equations
  • Geometry
  • Statistics and Probability
Each lesson includes video explanations, practice problems, and downloadable worksheets.
